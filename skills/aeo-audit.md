# AEO Audit

The AEO audit skill helps evaluate and improve visibility in AI-generated search answers and answer engines.

## Use Cases

- Audit a brand, website, article, offer, video, or content plan for AI answer visibility.
- Improve the odds of being mentioned, cited, or accurately summarized by answer engines.
- Compare visibility across Google AI features, ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, Copilot, Gemini, and similar platforms.
- Identify gaps in content coverage, entity clarity, authority signals, freshness, and third-party mentions.

## What It Looks At

- Query and prompt patterns people may use.
- Query fan-out topics and adjacent subquestions.
- Search fundamentals that still affect AI retrieval.
- Citation-ready content structure.
- Consensus, authority, freshness, and third-party mentions.
- YouTube and media visibility.
- Technical crawlability and AI crawler access.
- Measurement signals such as AI referrals, bot activity, self-reported attribution, and share of voice.
- Misinformation risk.
