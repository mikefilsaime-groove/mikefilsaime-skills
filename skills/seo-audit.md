# SEO Audit

The SEO audit skill reviews public-facing webpages for search optimization, crawlability, and modern web quality signals.

## Use Cases

- Review whether a page follows current SEO best practices.
- Check title tags, meta descriptions, canonicals, robots directives, and heading structure.
- Review content quality, internal links, image optimization, accessibility basics, and structured data.
- Identify technical blockers that may affect indexing, rankings, or search presentation.

## What It Looks At

- Critical SEO elements.
- Content structure and search intent alignment.
- Technical SEO and Core Web Vitals.
- Mobile friendliness.
- Security and crawlability.
- Schema.org structured data.
- Open Graph and social sharing metadata.
- SEO-impacting accessibility issues.
